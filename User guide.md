# Alarm Clock App – User Guide

## 1. Introduction
The Alarm Clock app allows users to set alarms for daily activities such as waking up, reminders, or scheduled tasks.
This guide explains how to use the basic features of the Alarm Clock app.

This guide is intended for all mobile phone users.

---

## 2. Supported Devices
The Alarm Clock app is available on:
- Android smartphones
- iPhones (iOS)

---

## 3. Opening the Alarm Clock App

### Steps
1. Unlock your mobile phone.
2. Locate the **Clock** or **Alarm** app on your device.
3. Tap the app icon to open it.

The home screen displays existing alarms (if any).

---

## 4. Setting a New Alarm

### Steps
1. Open the **Alarm Clock** app.
2. Tap the **Add (+)** button.
3. Select the desired **hour** and **minutes**.
4. Choose **AM** or **PM** (if applicable).
5. Tap **Save**.

The alarm is now set and enabled.

---

## 5. Editing an Existing Alarm

### Steps
1. Open the **Alarm Clock** app.
2. Tap the alarm you want to edit.
3. Modify the time or settings as needed.
4. Tap **Save**.

Changes take effect immediately.

---

## 6. Enabling or Disabling an Alarm

### Steps
1. Open the **Alarm Clock** app.
2. Use the toggle switch next to the alarm:
   - **On** to enable
   - **Off** to disable

Disabled alarms will not ring.

---

## 7. Deleting an Alarm

### Steps
1. Open the **Alarm Clock** app.
2. Press and hold the alarm you want to delete.
3. Tap **Delete** or the trash icon.

The alarm is permanently removed.

---

## 8. Alarm Sound and Vibration

### Changing Alarm Sound
1. Tap an existing alarm.
2. Select **Alarm Sound**.
3. Choose a sound from the list.
4. Tap **Save**.

### Vibration
- Enable vibration if you want the phone to vibrate when the alarm rings.

---

## 9. When the Alarm Rings

When the alarm rings:
- Tap **Stop** to turn it off
- Tap **Snooze** to delay the alarm by a few minutes

---

## 10. Common Issues

| Issue | Solution |
|------|---------|
| Alarm did not ring | Check volume and alarm status |
| Sound too low | Increase device volume |
| Alarm disabled | Enable the alarm toggle |

---

## 11. Best Practices

- Set alarms a few minutes earlier than required
- Check alarm volume before sleeping
- Avoid enabling airplane mode if sound is required
- Keep your phone charged overnight
